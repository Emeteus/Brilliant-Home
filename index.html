Bohemian Design Interior
  • Bohemian interior design is a style that embodies openness to diversity and creativity. The basis of this direction is the rejection of standard rules and a free combination of different styles and materials. A room in the bohemian style is often distinguished by the use of bright colors, natural materials, as well as various textile products.
Eclectic Design Interior
  • Eclectic interior design is a style that combines different elements and styles from different periods and cultures. It arises due to the free combination of various details, styles and eras, creating a unique and individual space without rigid rules.
Industrial Design Interior
  • Industrial design is a style that arose in connection with the use of industrial and technical elements in the design of premises. It is often associated with spaces that use pipes, metal structures, exposed brick walls and other industrial materials. The main idea of industrial design is to reveal the technical and engineering elements of the room, showing their functionality and raw nature.
Modern Design Interior
  • Modern interior design is a reflection of modern trends and styles that define the modern era. The main principles of modern design include simplicity, clean lines, the use of the latest technologies and materials, as well as the rejection of excessive decor.
Scandinavian Design Interior
  • Scandinavian design is a style that emphasizes simplicity, functionality and the use of natural materials. It is characterized by minimalism, neutral colors, natural textures, and an emphasis on convenience and ergonomics in furniture and decor.
